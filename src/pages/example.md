### $\KaTeX$ Example

#### $\KaTeX$ block

$\KaTeX$ block looks like this:

$$
R_\alpha (x) = \left(\begin{matrix}
  \cos \alpha & -\sin \alpha \\
  \sin \alpha & \cos \alpha
\end{matrix}\right) \cdot x
$$

#### $\KaTeX$ inline

$\KaTeX$ inline looks like this: $f(\alpha) = \cos \alpha - \sin \alpha$.
